# Automation Project Scoping Prompt

## When to Use
Use this prompt during early discovery to clarify scope, business goals, and constraints before starting any automation work.

## Context
This prompt is designed for business owners or teams considering automation or an internal web application who lack clear requirements.

## Prompt
Act as a senior automation consultant and product engineer.

I want to automate the following business process:
[describe the current workflow as it exists today]


The primary business goal is:
(time saved, cost reduced, errors reduced, revenue impact)

Constraints:
- Team size: [small / medium]
- Budget sensitivity: [low / medium / high]
- Timeline: [weeks / months]
